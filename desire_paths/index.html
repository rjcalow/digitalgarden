Desire Paths

"desire lines" - paths & tracks made over time by the wishes & feet of walkers, especially those paths that run contrary to design or planning. Free-will ways. Aka "cow-paths" & "Olifantenpad" (elephant trails).

Robert Macfarlane (2018)
