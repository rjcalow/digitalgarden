Inadequate biography: I am Richard. I usually go by @rjcalow. I am an artist-photographer, maker, writer focused on culture-nature dichotomy and overlooked spaces.

Proposition: while rjcalow.co.uk is a portfolio, this is my digital sketchbook, a personal wiki, a digital garden of notes. Given its intermediate state, it should be considered incomplete, subject to change and as work in progress.

Regarding design and specification: the internet is bloated and bad for the environment, not to mention rife with spyware and walled gardens. Inspired by Low tech magazine and 512kb.club, this garden strives to be a minimalist DIY approach and homage to the personal homepage -from a time when the internet was fun.

Navigating the garden: if using the index seems archaic, you may wish to use the javascript powered search.
