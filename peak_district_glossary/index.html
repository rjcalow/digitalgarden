Peak District Glossary

Local dialects in the geography of the Peak, (mostly dark).

Clough

  • Valley or ravine.
    • i.e. Grindsbrook Clough

Dark peak

  • Northern Peak district
  • Named after the dark gritstone.

Knoll

  • A hill or mound.
    • i.e. Grindslow Knoll

Mam

  • Breast shaped
    • i.e. Mam Tor

Owler

  • An alder tree.
    • i.e. Owler Tor.

Shaw

  • Strip of woodland.
    • i.e. Longshaw, near Hathersage.

Tor

  • Rock formation

White Peak

  • Named after the white limestone.
