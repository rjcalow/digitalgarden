Snag / Inflorescence

About

Synopsis: A unique nature-human relationship within a marginal space: a transitional border between a neglected rose garden and a new wilder space where the existing cultivated nature grows uncontrolled alongside opportunistic self-seeding nature.

The work is an amalgamation of legacy -historic human influence on land, and marginal spaces -sites of transition outside of conventional urban circuits.

Produced over a COVID restricted masters, the outcomes took the form of a photobook/zine rather than a wall display. This benefited the project allowing for sequencing and narrative exploration. The book consists of 28 images over 48 pages including cover.
